Privacy Policy for Isolat

Effective Date: May 25, 2025

Isolat is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Location Data

We collect your device's precise GPS location to:

  • Determine your prayer times based on your current time zone and region.
  • Show the Qibla direction accurately from your current location.

This data is processed locally on your device and is not stored or shared with any third party.

2. Notifications

We use local notifications to alert you when it is time for each daily prayer. These notifications are generated and managed directly on your device.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Calculate accurate prayer times for your location.
  • Display the correct Qibla direction.
  • Notify you of prayer times via device notifications.

We do not collect or share personal information. Your location data is used only during app usage and not stored.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use external APIs to fetch accurate prayer times. These services do not receive your personal data or location.

5. Data Retention

We do not store any personal or location data on our servers. All calculations and notifications are handled locally on your device.

6. Your Consent

By using Isolat, you consent to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.
